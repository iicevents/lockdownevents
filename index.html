LIST OF EVENTS AND SUBMISSIONS RULES

GROUP-A

Who can participate? : All the students (UG, PG, Ph.D) of KEC

Theme:Creating Awareness for COVID-19

Event Code: A01

Name Of The Event: Poster Making

Submission Mode: Facebook

Rules:

  • Either individual or a team of 3 members can participate
  • Create a poster of A4 size based on the theme

Event Code: A02

Name Of The Event: Slogan

Submission Mode: Online (G-Form)

Rules:

  • Either individual or a team of 3 members can participate
  • Create an impressive slogan based on the theme

Event Code: A03

Name Of The Event: Creative Writing

Submission Mode: Online (G-Form)

Rules:

  • Unlike essay writing, students can write their creative views for not more than 2 pages (either Tamil or English) based on the theme
  • Individuals can participate and upload their articles through online
  • Evaluation will be made based on uniqueness/contentand plagiarism report
  • This event will be helpful in writing research articles of their own without plagiarism

Event Code: A04

Name Of The Event: Creative Talk

Submission Mode: Facebook

Rules:

  • Unlike elocution or speech or discussion, students can express their creative views for not more than 2 minutes (either Tamil or English) based on the theme
  • Individuals can participate and upload their recording through online
  • Evaluation will be made based on pronouncation/vocabulary and the ability to express the view
  • This event will be helpful in placement activities

Event Code: A05

Name Of The Event: Creative Tik-Tok

Submission Mode: Facebook

Rules:

  • Students can submit an impressive tik-tok videos based on the theme
  • Respective students submitting the videos should feature in the video and it should be only based on the theme
  • Either individual or a team of 3 members can participate

Event Code: A06

Name Of The Event: Creative Memes

Submission Mode: Facebook

Rules:

  • Students can submit an impressive meme (either image or video) based on the theme
  • Either individual or a team of 3 members can participate

GROUP-B

Who can participate? : All the students (UG, PG, Ph.D) of KEC

Theme:Science and Technology for Societal need (Any sectors: energy, agriculture, healthcare, defence, water and sanitation, rural development etc.,)

Event Code: B01

Name Of The Event: Idea Submission (OWN)

Submission Mode: Online (G-Form)

Rules:

  • Either individual or a team of 3 members can participate
  • Students can submit any innovative novel ideas based on the problem solving for the theme specified
  • Submit an idea with a title, abstract (250 words),block diagram and methodology
  • Submission should be not more than 1.5 pages

Event Code: B02

Name Of The Event: Idea Submission (SIH)

Submission Mode: Online (G-Form)

Rules:

  • Either individual or a team of 3 members can participate
  • Don’t you have any own ideas for solving a societalproblem? Don’t worry!
  • Visit SIH website and check for the problems specified
  • Choose any problem and submit an idea
  • Submit an ideas with a title, abstract (250 words), block diagram and methodology
  • Submission should be not more than 1.5 pages

Event Code: B03

Name Of The Event: Idea Submission (INDUSTRY)

Submission Mode: Online (G-Form)

Rules:

  • Either individual or a team of 3 members can participate
  • Don’t you have any own ideas for solving a societalproblem? Or don’t you find any problems falling onto your interest in SIH? Don’t worry!
  • Collect the problem from an industry (run by your parents or relatives or friends etc.,)
  • Choose a problem and submit an idea
  • Submit an idea with a title, abstract (250 words),block diagram and methodology
  • Submission should be not more than 1.5 pages

Theme:No theme... It’s a fun time with family!!

Submission Mode: Online (G-Form)

  • Events: tik tok videos, singing with family, cooking with family, creative awareness message to the public, farming with family etc.,

Event Code: C02

Name Of The Event: Creativity of Individual

Submission Mode: Online (G-Form)

Rules:

  • Except the student of KEC, other members in the family can participate in C02 event

Event Code: C03

Name Of The Event: Institute Industry Connect (IIC)

Submission Mode: Online (G-Form)

Rules:

  • If your parents run an industry (any industry) or an entrepreneur or a farmer, you are eligible to participate!
  • They can post their technical and scientific problems faced in their industry
  • The problems will be given to the KEC students and the solutions arrived will be shared to the industry
  • Evaluation is complete if the solution is satisfied by the industry
  • All the industry takes part in this event will be rewarded
